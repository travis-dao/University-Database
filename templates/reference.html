Reference

School Name

The name of the univerisity of college. There might be variations at the end due to multiple campuses.

Admissions Rate

The percentage of students admitted out of all applicants

Location

Location of the school. It goes "City, State (Geographic Region)".

Local Population Size

Population size of the local area.

Carnegie Classification - Basic

Following the "Carnegie Commision on Higher Education" framework, this describes what type of university it is. Example: Doctoral Universities: Very High Research Activity. More specifically, the focus and level of education. For more information, click this.

Carnegie Classification - Size

Following the "Carnegie Commision on Higher Education" framework, this includes the enrollment period and residential character. Example: Four-year, large, highly residential. For more information, click this.

Religious Affiliation

If the university is religious. If not, then "n/a" is written instead.

Undergrad Size

How many undergraduate students there are.

Program Distribution

The spread of chosen education programs. These are more general so multiple majors can be included per grouping.

Ethnic Distribution

Ethnic diveristy. (Affirmative Action?)

SAT / ACT

Standardized test scores at the 25th, 50th, and 75th percentile. Note that in recent years, test submissions are optional so results are not 100% representative. If test scores are not given by schools, then "n/a" is written instead.

Federal Loan

How many students took out a federal student loan.

Tuition Cost

Tuition costs for in-state and out-of-state students. Public schools tend to have different prices while privates school tend to have the same prices.

Average Attendance Cost & Costs Per Income Range

The average price a student will pay. This includes tuition & fees, textbooks & supplies, living expenses; minus grant/scholarship aid. Calculated per income group. Negative numbers means no fees â€” could be full-ride scholarship and more.

Miscellaneous Costs

Fees other than tuition. Includes: residence, supplies.
